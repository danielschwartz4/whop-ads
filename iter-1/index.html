Questions, answered

Do I need my own website or tech setup?

No. Set up your storefront, list your coaching offers, and share your link — Whop handles checkout, billing, and access. No code.

Can I sell recurring memberships?

Yes. Subscriptions and recurring billing are built in, so you can run monthly coaching, communities, and memberships out of the box.

I already run Meta ads — can I bring my account?

Yes. Once approved for Whop Ads you connect your existing ad account, page, and pixel via OAuth — connecting your pixel preserves its learnings and event history.

What does it cost?

Creating your business is free; Whop earns a fee only when you make a sale. Whop Ads has no platform, setup, monthly, or minimum-spend fees — you only pay for the ad spend itself.

What if my ad account gets banned?

Whop's agency accounts run at Meta's highest tier, so rejections are rarer — and if you're ever restricted, you get a same-day account replacement instead of days of downtime.
